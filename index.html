Happy Birthday, Koalaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaa. 🐨

I don't know how to write this. I've never been good at saying things out loud — but you already know that about me. So let me try, maybe for the first time properly.

You were there when I had no one. Not just "there" — you actually listened. To my stupid stuff like GTA, my favourite movies, series, Avengers, my 2am thoughts, my bad days that had no reason, my good days that needed someone to share with. You became the person I didn't know I needed. Quietly, without announcing it.

I remember every moment I have spent with you. I remember dropping you home in the middle of winter, both of us shivering and somehow still laughing. I remember the little things — the way you listened without ever making me feel like a burden, the way you showed up even when I gave you no reason to. The way talking to you made everything feel lighter, like the noise in my head just... stopped.

And somewhere in between all of that, I kept taking it for granted.

I walked away. Again and again. And every time, I only thought about what I was going through — never about what it felt like on your side. What it took to come back each time. What kind of strength that actually is. I didn't see it then. I see it now, and I'm ashamed it took me this long.

I'm sorry. Not the easy kind. The kind where I actually sit with how much I must have hurt you and feel the weight of it. No excuses. Just — I'm sorry.

But here's the thing I can't explain away. Whenever my world falls apart, I don't reach for anyone else. I reach for you. Not because of any label, not because we're "supposed to" — just because somewhere inside me, you are the place that feels like calm. Like everything gets quieter. Like it's going to be okay.

I always feel like I have to protect you from everything. Your reactions to things are unlike anyone I've ever met. Talking to you makes my chest lighter in a way nothing else does. And when things get really bad — you're still the first person my mind runs to. Not a choice. Just where I go.

That's not habit. That's not convenience. That's you being something real to me, in a way I don't have words for.

So happy birthday, Koala. 🐨 I hope this year, life is as soft with you as you have always been with me.

— Yours, always. Even when I was stupid enough to pretend otherwise.
